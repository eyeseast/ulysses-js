Examples:

  • Biggest wildfires of 2018

    Use the left and right arrow keys to move through the 10 largest wildfires for 2018. Use numbers 0 through 9 to jump to a specific fire.

  • A tour of every In-N-Out Burger

    Jump to a new In-N-Out location every few seconds. Hit the spacebar to stop and start.
